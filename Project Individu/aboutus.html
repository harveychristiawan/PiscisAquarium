Piscis Aquarium


Piscis Aquarium is a leading marine animal conservation company providing experiences that matter and inspiring guests to protect animals and the wild marine wonders of our world. We are one of the world's foremost zoological organizations and a global leader in animal husbandry, behavioral management, veterinary care and animal welfare. We also rescue and rehabilitate marine and terrestrial animals that are ill, injured, orphaned or abandoned, with the goal of returning them to the wild.

The Piscis Aquarium® rescue teams have helped more than 50,000 animals in need over the last 100 years. Over our more than 100-year history, we have built a diversified portfolio of 10 destination and regional animal conservation that are grouped in key markets across 12 countries. Many of our tanks showcase our one-of-a-kind zoological collection and all of our tanks feature a diverse array of marine biological creatures with broad demographic appeal which deliver memorable experiences and a strong value proposition for our guests.

We have also produced Parent's Choice and Emmy Award winning shows like Marine Rescue that celebrate real & inspirational stories of rescue, rehabilitation and return to the wild -- by the animal rescue, animal care and veterinary teams at Piscis Aquarium, in cooperation with our partners also committed to care for marine wildlife in need.
